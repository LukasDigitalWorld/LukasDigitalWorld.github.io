The ICP BOI color NFT

I love learning about cryptocurrencies and the new technologies that comes with it. One of the resulting technologies is the NFT.

NFT stands for “Non-Fungible Token”, which means a digital asset that cannot be exchanged. For example, you can upload an image and then mint it on any blockchain to make it "immortalize". The first NFT hype broke out in 2021. Many will remember the monkey pictures that were sold for millions on digital art markets back then.
